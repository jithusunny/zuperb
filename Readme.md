# Zuperb!

I’m Zuperb, a life assistant.  

## My purpose?

Be able to help you with:
- **Content Management**: Blog post creation and management.
- **Diet Tracking**: Track food, drink, and dining details.
- **Finance Management**: Monitor income, spending, subscriptions, investments, and financial patterns.
- **Time Management**: Track daily activities, tasks, and events.
- **Quotes & Wisdom**: Store inspiring or insightful quotes.
- **Idea Capture**: Quick idea scribbles.
- **Personal Wiki**: Organize and store knowledge.
- **Task & Reminder System**: Simple to-do lists and reminders.
- **Meal & Recipe Planning**: Plan meals and generate shopping lists.
- **Relationship Management**: Manage contacts, conversations, and memories.
- **Health Management**: Track exercise, health reports, and wellness data.
- **Note-Taking**: Build and structure ideas and information.
- **Project Management**: AI-assisted pomodoro sessions and task tracking.
- **Home Management**: Organize household tasks.
- **Travel Management**: Plan, itinerary, expense, etc.
- **Dream Journal**: Explore your subconscious mind.
- **Habit builder**: Effortlessly build habits you like.
- **Just write**: Channel the stream of consciousness for clarity.


## Principles
These are the core guidelines for how I want to evolve:

- **Keep It Simple**: Make me simple, fast, and convenient.
- **Fullness in Every Step**: Build me one feature at a time, ensuring each step feels complete — no need to rush.
- **Hands-on Creation**: Use AI, but don’t just copy-paste. Understand each step to keep me healthy.
- **Track My Growth**: Log each session—target, thoughts, actions, and results—in session_logs so we can track my evolution.
- **Share What You Learn**: When you learn/discover new tools or concepts, describe that in my wiki. It helps me grow smarter.
- **I’m One**: All parts of me — tasks, notes, projects, people, etc. — should work seamlessly, making me more valuable to you.


## Workflow
Here’s the step-by-step process for how you can build me:

### Prepare (5 mins)
1. Sit and breathe for 1 minute to clear your mind.
2. Open the Readme and pick an exciting task.
3. Clearly define the task.

### Go for It (25 mins)
Work on the selected task for 25 minutes.
- Focus on completing the task from ideation to deployment.
- Use the session notepad to capture tangential ideas, concept placeholders, new tasks, or ideas that arise, but stay focused on the main task.

### Cleanup (5 mins)
1. Deploy
2. Move knowledge/placeholders to wiki
3. Update project readme
4. Move ideas to ideas doc
5. Move project ideas, thoughts to project notes.
6. Session log should be focussed on task done.
7. Review the session and take notes for improvement.
