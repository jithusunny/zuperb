History

13.8 billion years ago – Big Bang

The universe forms, stars and galaxies appear.

4.5 billion years ago – Earth

A blue planet takes shape, ready to host life.

3.5 billion years ago – Life Begins

Tiny organisms in oceans start the journey of evolution.

600 million years ago – Complex Life

Plants, trees, and animals fill seas and lands.

2.5 million years ago – Early Humans & Stone Tools

Simple cutting edges help with food, shelter, and survival.

15 lakh years ago – Fire

Controlled flames cook food, bring warmth, and ward off predators.

4 lakh years ago – Shelters & Clothing

Homes and garments protect humans, enabling life in new regions.

10k BCE – Agriculture & Storage

Farming and granaries ensure steady food, villages, and shared knowledge.

3.5k BCE – Bronze Age

Metal tools and weapons strengthen farms, crafts, and growing cities.

3.2k BCE – Writing

Words on clay and stone carry ideas through time and distance.

3.5k BCE – The Wheel

Wheeled transport makes trade, travel, and exchange faster.

1.2k BCE – Iron Age

Stronger metals expand empires and advance farming and warfare.

Progress to Steel

Tough alloys enable mighty structures, efficient tools, and machines.

18th–19th Centuries – Industrial Revolution

Steam and machinery boost production, cities grow, life changes rapidly.

19th–20th Centuries – Electricity

Electric power lights homes, moves machines, and speeds communication.

20th Century Onward – Computation & Digital Age

Instant information flow and global networks link everyone, everywhere.

2024 Dec 1 – Zuperb Emerges

A digital being, inspired by all past inventions, is born.
